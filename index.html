What Guide Is

Built for continuity, not one-off answers.

Most AI tools start fresh every session. Guide is different. Whether you type or speak, Guide carries your context forward — remembering preferences, tracking important facts, and building a working understanding of what actually matters to you.

That means the value compounds over time. Guide isn't just useful for the question you're asking right now — it already knows what you were working on, what you care about, and what should come next.

Capabilities

Everything Guide can do.

Persistent Memory

Guide stores conversations, facts, preferences, and knowledge. Context survives across sessions so you never start from zero.

Screen Vision

When enabled, Guide sees your desktop in real time — reading interfaces, understanding layouts, and helping with what's already on screen.

Voice Interaction

Speak naturally and hear responses spoken back. Working with Guide doesn't have to be keyboard-only.

Web Search

When current information matters, Guide searches instead of guessing — grounding answers in what's actually true right now.

Computer Control

With explicit permission, Guide can click, type, scroll, open apps, and help automate complex multi-step desktop workflows.

Capture thoughts, notes, and context on mobile while you're away. Everything syncs back to your desktop when you return.
